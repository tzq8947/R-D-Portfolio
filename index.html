Welcome to our R&D Digital Portfolio

The goal of this project is to develop an Android application proof of concept for NIWA to crowd-source data on heating emmissions from wood and coal burning in households.

Read More Learn More

Main features of our Woodsmoke Monitoring App

Air Quality Map

Check air qualtiy information near you using data pulled from the World Quality Index.

Smoke Incident Map

Check to see if users have reported any smoke incidents near you.

Dashboard Visualisations

Have a look at our various fun visualisations on smoke report and air quality data on your home page.

Report Smoke Incident

Allows you to report a smoke incident anywhere and anytime.

Report history

View your past reporting history here.

Information Page

Why was the research and upskilling phase important?

The research phase allowed everyone on the team to become more informed about our client's needs and how we can help them effectively tackle the issue of crowd-sourcing data on home heating emmissions. As for the upskilling phase, this was very important as it ensured that everyone on the team is adeaquately equipped with the knowledge to develop the product.
